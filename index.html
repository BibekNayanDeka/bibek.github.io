Hello.

I study the sounds of language — how they are structured, perceived, and organized across different languages.

I'm interested in questions like:

  • How are speech sounds structured and distributed?
  • How are they perceived and processed?
  • What patterns emerge across languages, and how are they organized?
  • Can these patterns be predicted using symbolic models?
  • Can we understand how neural models learn these patterns?
  • Is domain-informed neural modelling worth it?

Research Interests

  • Phonological theory
  • Intonational phonology and prosody
  • Computational modeling of linguistic structures
  • Speech processing
